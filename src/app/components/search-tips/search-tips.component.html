Welcome to the browse page! Here you will find the companies where you will invest your credit. If any of the results match your exact search, you'll see them highlighted below. Use the following tips to begin exploring the market:

Stock ticker symbols are unique abbreviations assigned to companies, such as AAPL for Apple or TSLA for Tesla. If you know the ticker symbol, simply type it into the search bar for direct results.

You can also search using the full or partial name of the company. For example, typing "Microsoft" will show relevant results even if you don’t know the ticker symbol.

If you’re exploring stocks in a particular industry or sector, try using keywords like “technology,” “healthcare,” or “energy”. This can help you discover companies you might not have considered.

Sometimes companies or industries are listed under alternative names or synonyms. If your first search doesn’t yield results, try variations of the term.
