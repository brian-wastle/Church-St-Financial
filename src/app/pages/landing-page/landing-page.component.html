Your Investing Journey Starts Here

Church St. Financial is an interactive learning platform designed for students to explore the exciting world of stock trading in a fun and risk-free environment. Whether you're new to investing or looking to sharpen your skills, this is the perfect place to start.

When you sign up, you'll receive $100,000 in Church St. Credit (CSC), a virtual currency you can use to buy and sell shares. Build your portfolio, track daily performance, and gain real-worldexperience—all without spending a dime.

Use our Browse page to discover new stocks. If a company catches your eye, dive deeper into their story on their company page to learn more about their business.

With Church St. Financial, you'll gain valuable insights into how the stock market works, make strategic decisions, and see how your investments grow over time. It's not just about trading—it's about building confidence and understanding the financial world in a safe and supportive environment.



Start your journey today and unlock the tools to become a smarter, more informed investor.
